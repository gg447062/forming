FORMING

July 23, 2022

Presented By Lexicon Devils

About

An experimental hyperverse concert as the pinnacle for Lexicon Devil’s monthly event series with Juicebox.money. Featuring curated lineups of web3 musicians, we will share original recording of their performances at a virtual party inside the Juicebox parcel on Voxels. We want to demonstrate the ease and capability of Juicebox to be used as a tool to demonstrate how radical transparency can facilitate creative production and give other Web3 artists a sip of what Juicebox and Voxels can offer for their Web3 stack. Both DJ & live sets are welcome submissions, as well as the use of visualizers, video edits, or any visual representation an artist prefers.
